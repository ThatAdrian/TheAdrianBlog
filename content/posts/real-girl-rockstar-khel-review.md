## The Sound

Honestly this album is sick, it’s like hertz which was already quite decent but this one has a bit more of a refined sound and style.

The tracks are all very bouncy and digicorey and khel has a great flow on all of them making it a great combination in general.

On first listen I feel like I like this album a bit more than hertz but we’ll have to see if it stands the test of time.

Also she sampled pinkpantheress?????
