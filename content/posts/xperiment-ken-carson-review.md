## The Sound

I've never been a huge fan of ken carson. i stand by it. this album was boring as hell, all the beats melded in to eachother, nothing interesting in a single one of those. the vocals are the exact same tone and flow as there has been since he first released anything. the only mention worthy part was the cover or whatever youd call the 2hollis track. of course im a 2hollis fan though so yeah. not much more to say. the beat on knocking is funky but still same boring flow and vocals.
