## The Sound

Unfortunately quite a mid album, there's some bops on it but the tracks are quite repetitive and the replayability is a bit low on this one, not much difference from the previous album from kuru which is a shame cause i think he's a cool guy and a nice hairdresser but yeah the tracks are all kinda the nowadays equivalent of mumble rap where its just slurred speech with tons of autotune on out of key beats. im sorry. the lows on this album are kinda low but there are some decent tracks. I like the tung tung tung sahur line haha. I heard FW19 when that first released as a single and i was excited as that is a great track and then Noir Kei came out after that and that one was also decent so i had my hopes up, it might just be me tho.

## Standout Tracks

FW19, Noir Kei and Like Glue

## Verdict

A repetitive listen, I'll probably listen to a few of these tracks again and a bunch of it will show up on shuffle but nothing special from this one unfortunately.
