## The Sound

Is that actually Mr Fetty Wap playing the piano and using violins?? This album is enjoyable, it's simple r & b and hip-hop/rap as expected, there's a lot of variety in sound throughout it, and I think it's well-made. The only reason some of my points are low here is that those tracks don't stand out; they're a bit boring, but there are some catchy, smooth, and nice to listen to tracks. I was surprised to hear so many of the acoustic parts in this, which were smooth. Fetty's voice is very nice, and he does well as an r & b artist.

The marketing for this album was below average. I'm sure most people didn't even know this guy still makes music, needs some investing there. This guy is buff as hell btw, just looking at the Spotify video that's playing as I listen. 

It sounds like he covered a few songs in this album also? strange but ok.

## Verdict

An okay R&B and Rap album, it's about what you would expect, it doesn't live up to the self-titled album, but I don't hate this one.
