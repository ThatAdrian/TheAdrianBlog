## The Sound

Is that actually Mr Fetty Wap playing the piano and using violins?? This album is enjoyable, theres a lot of variety in sound throughout it and i think its well made, the only reason some of my points are low here is because those tracks dont stand out, theyre a bit boring but there are some catchy smooth and nice to listen to tracks. I was surprised to hear so many of the acoustic parts in this, which were smooth. Fetty's voice is very nice, and he does well as an r& b artist.

The marketing for this album was below average. I'm sure most people didn't even know this guy still makes music, needs some investing there. This guy is buff as hell btw, just looking at the Spotify video that's playing as I listen.
