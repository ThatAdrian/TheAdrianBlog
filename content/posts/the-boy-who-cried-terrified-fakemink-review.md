## The Sound

Its an okay EP, not revolutionary, but this seems as if this is only something fakemink used as a marketing tool for his upcoming self-produced album, Terrified. The first two songs are quite catchy. Mink tends to use the same style and flow throughout each song which gets bland when you hear them all in a row, I think. On its own, I think it's decent at most. I'm looking forward to the whole upcoming album to see how he produces when he puts all of himself into it.

[TRACK_RATINGS]

## Standout Tracks

Blow the speaker and young millionaire

## Verdict

Overall decent, nothing crazy and I believe his singles were better prior to this. I'm looking forward to Terrified coming out soon.
