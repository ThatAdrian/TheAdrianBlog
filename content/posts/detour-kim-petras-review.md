## The Sound

This album is quite decent. I first heard of it through the Frost Children Instagram, but then got convinced to listen to it after Emwell posted about it, glad I did.

This album is a quite a solid electronic selection for the most part. There are some skips though towards the halfway mark of the total. I hate the song brutalist and then there are a couple of mediocre ones like jeep and bitch ball out but it's okay cause the good songs make up for it.

If I were to compare this album to something, it would be like the slayyyter album but more creative, more enjoyable and more unique.

I first heard some of this album when Jane Remover sampled Polo on #boyletmeknow on the Leroy side project, and it was a bit of a neuron activation when I heard it.

## Standout Tracks

101, Need for Speed, DTLA and Check it

## Verdict

Overall solid project, will be adding a hefty handful onto my playlist and its tempting me to pull out the DJ mixer with these songs to be honest. Will keep an eye out as I wasn't even aware of the singles prior to this.
