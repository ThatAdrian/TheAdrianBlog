## The Sound

This is a very solid release from malcolm, ive listened to it a handful of times and its quite good. not as good as sweet boy and maybe on par with the self titled, no complaints from me. 

This album has a very simple pop sound but he does it well, I think he did Sweet Boy with a bit more pizzazz and a bit less generic pop sound and maybe they were a bit more consistently catchy.

The songs on this album are all quite good, the main interests are the singles of the album which are bundled together in this tracklist so there's a bit of an increase and then a slight decrease in rating after them but they're super good songs and I believe Malcolm is super talented and good at what he does.

When first listening to this album, they seem all quite generic but after hearing them all separately, whether it's on the internet or on shuffle, you notice that they're all individually really good and there's a consistency rather than repetitiveness.

## Standout Tracks

Breathe, I saw your face, free.99 and difficult love

## Verdict

Very solid album, I'm surprised he's not up the charts yet even with this release, i think the next one will definitely get him up there because hes very consistent with the quality of his songs. Go listen to it, I do prefer sweet boy though.
