## The Sound

Hi so this album isnt horrible but also not amazing, its mid, it has some good tracks but after the first half it really goes down hill and just sounds like sloppy cyberpunk radio music. i despise tracks like im actually kinda famous and yes god. yuck they make me cringe. then theres some ive actually heard on the radio which was a nice surprise but it awoke me to realising how generic and pop slop filled some of the songs are like brittany murphy. dont get me wrong, again, its not horrid but the only reason i say that is because the first few tracks are okay. i think tracks like dance, beat up chanels and old technology are actually more unique and enjoyable compared to the rest.

I found Slayyyter through shuffle when old technology came on, thats a good song. i havent listened to the rest of the albums at the time im writing this.
