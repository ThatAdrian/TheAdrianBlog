## The Sound

This album is a topic i could go on about for ages, i have no clue how i discovered it but ive been listening to it regularly and i had the full album on loop since probaly november 2025 and i actually believe it has degraded over time and its not at all me growing out of it and i can explain now.

at first it released and it was perfect, then it got taken down

then it came back but missing a couple tracks such as 24/7 and power 4. this ruined the transitions of the tracks, then it got reworked to work without them which still didnt make the transitions hit right. etc etc. on the final version that we have now we are missing power 4 even though it has been released as a single which is disappointing because the transition between the tracks around it still doesnt go as perfectly as it did into the original track.

This album i disliked at first believe it or not, i thought it was sloppy rage rap with mumbly lyrics but listening to it more and more i got even more and more stuff stuck in my head and i started to appreciate the amazing production from Waera and slayr which has such a refined melodic sound and so many iconic leads and what not.
