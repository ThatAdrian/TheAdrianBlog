## The Sound

Lovely simple album with some cool digicore beats, super funky, bouncy and enjoyable. I found khel through some simple Spotify shuffle a while ago and meant to listen to the album fully but haven't until now. I like the aesthetic and all. decent listen in general but nothing revolutionary.

Does she know?, super duper and steppin

## Verdict

Listen to it, its decent, sounds a bit like lucy bedroque before unmusique, ill keep up with releases, i think they have potential.
