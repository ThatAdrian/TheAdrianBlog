## The Sound

A classic, can't go wrong with this album. Dare I say it is the best Daft Punk album, with Ram being a very close runner-up.

Being made in 2001, this album reshaped music with tracks like One More Time and Harder Better Faster Stronger. It still pulls its weight super well and i tend to relisten very often. it most definitely created an outline for electronic music and cemented the duo in the hall of fame.

If you asked me a couple of months ago, I would've said it's a 10/10, but some tracks aren't as good as other ones, not to say they're bad but you can't tell me Night Vision or Short Circuit are as good as Something About Us.

## Standout Tracks

Verdis Quo, Something about us, Digital Love and one more time

## Verdict

It's a must listen. It's great and a staple. Go listen if you haven't, but you most likely have.
