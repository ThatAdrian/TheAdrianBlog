## The Sound

This one's more what I was looking for compared to Habibti, quite bouncy, more variety and more energy. IS THAT TEEZO TOUCHDOWN ON ROADTRIPS??? Anyways, it's a lot more fun, cheetah print is completely stupid, but it's funny by all means, why the hell is sexyy red doing the chacha slide? I can see this one being used in some DJ mixes online. It's catchy and entertaining. complete opposite of the other album. Still, I think its definitley nowhere near his best work, it gets a bit boring towards the end.

I think this album will have a similar effect to how Honestly, Nevermind wasn't that huge on its own but there are like 2-3 songs which got used and abused on the radio and clubs. A lot of songs on this album have very similar energy to Sticky for example.

## Standout Tracks

Hoe Phase, Road Trips and Outside Tweaking

## Verdict

It'll do, as a surprise album along with the other one. This one is better than Habibti, but now my hopes are up for the actual Iceman album, which will be after this one.
