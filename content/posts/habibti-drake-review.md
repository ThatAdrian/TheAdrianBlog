## The Sound

This is one of his slower releases, closer to the PND album he released recently. its okay, it has some decent songs on it but most of it is slow and boring in my opinion. I'm not a huge fan of those songs.

Drake announced he's dropping Iceman on May 15th, and I woke up and there are 6 albums which are 3 different ones and then a clean version of each one which is insane.

I'm starting these reviews off with the two side albums, which were surprise released alongside ICEMAN first. I'm curious what the other two albums have in stock if we got the boring slow stuff out of the way with this one.
