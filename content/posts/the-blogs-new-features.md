### My Dashboard

I created a lovely dashboard for myself which makes it so much easier to post, make nice, high-quality, modular articles and music reviews of equal standing. 

### Song Previews

You can now preview the songs in my reviews using the little play button on the left side.

This uses Spotify to gather all the song data and information automatically when I put the Spotify URL on my dashboard, and then correlates that information to the song on Deezer, as Spotify stopped providing 30-second song previews a while ago.

### Community Ratings

You guys can now also join in with this!

You can, at the top of a music review, select your rating of the album, and it'll save directly to the community score; this is also updated on the music sub-page to allow you to switch between the community and my scores.

On top of that, you can also individually rate each track by pressing the little chat bubble to the right of the rack on a music review page. This will be shown when you post a track comment at the top of your box there.

### In-Line Comments

You can now leave comments!

This works by selecting some text, and a little chat bubble pops up where you can input your name and comment, and your comment will show up in a little icon on the right of the text.

You can also reply to other people's comments by pressing reply on a comment.

### Notifications

On the home page, at the top, there is a little bell icon in the top right.

If you press that, select the categories you're interested in, and then confirm in the email you receive (MAKE SURE TO CHECK YOUR SPAM!!), you'll be notified when I post!

### + MORE!!

Keep an eye out, and you'll find more and more interesting stuff popping up all over the place.

I'm constantly messing about with this and trying to make it better and better. I hope it all comes in more useful and enjoyable.
