## The Sound

I think Laufey is an amazing artist and possibly even in my top 10.

Her talent at both performing and composing is near unmatched and the way she is able to put emotion so strongly into her songs is incredible, with many of her songs being able to move me to tears.

Onto the album, it's a fantastic release. I listened to it in full when it originally came out and I believe I shed a tear at the time on the way to work. It's a very carefully composed work of art with no necessarily bad songs. I do think most of the notable songs are in the first half of the album but having 19 songs, I think that's enough to weigh the album out as a good album.

I would understand if someone called these songs samey as they are quite similar at some points, even I would have trouble telling some songs apart, even though they are all good in their own respect. The only reason I'm unable to give it a 5 is for that reason, and that it's not a perfect album as I'd be able to bundle a handful of songs and label them as filler.

## Standout Tracks

Tough Luck, Silver Lining and Lover Girl

## Verdict

Amazing and very emotional album which I recommend you to listen to, only reason it isn't a 5 is because there are a couple unmemorable, although good, songs.
