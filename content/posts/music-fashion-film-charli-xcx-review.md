## The Sound

It's an okay album. there is nothing that stands out here, i was borderline bored, it didnt keep me entertained throughout, the songs arent very different between them. i feel like brats success will be a very hard thing to live up to, wuthering heights was a decent bit better than this, i dont think anything stood out much to me in this album, a lot of charlis previous work was quite innovative and experimental, which I think has been washed away and switched to a bit of a generic pop sound nowadays.

## Standout Tracks

ss26, camera, 2007

## Verdict

I wouldve preferred a longer wait and a bigger more exciting build up considering we already had wuthering heights which was quite a decent album only 5 months ago. this one is whatever in my opinion.
