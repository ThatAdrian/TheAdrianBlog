## The Sound

This album has a special place in my heart,  I can't remember how I discovered it but it is such a harsh and raw album in some moments and some moments its the most hype music I've heard. I was tempted to rate all songs a 5 but that'd be unfair to the real standout tracks. 

When this first came out I was playing it on repeat but I'm quite sure they were completely different tracks to what I have on loop now. songs like Fuck, Bluebirds, I love you and it sounds stupid and Bennie and kay had me bawling my eyes out while foreigner, we don't leave the house and Asheville had me jumping. I could go on about this album for ages but I'll save you all the reading effort. 

Going to the live show was a game changer, I'm willing to say that at the point of me posting this that may have been the best show I've been to, everyone was so nice, setlist was great, I knew every song, I cried so many times and at one point I didn't stop throughout like 4 songs straight. It was incredible and I will be going as often as humanly possible.

## Standout Tracks

All of them but specifically: Fuck, Bennie & Kay, I love you and it sounds stupid, we don't leave the house and the two singles/first songs.

## Verdict

Highly recommend. Legendary album. 5/5
