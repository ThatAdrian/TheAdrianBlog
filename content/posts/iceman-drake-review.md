## The Sound

Literally the same reaction to when Certified Lover Boy came out, there's a lot of monotone, simple, boring, slow and repetitive rapping over slow beats. These take up most of the album. Those are mostly the 2-2.5s on this album.

After those, there is some rap with personality but it's not necessarily catchy in any way or anything revolutionary.

The feature distribution on this album is also strange, 3 artists, 2 on one song, and then 21 savage has literally 4 words that he says, I doubt that even counts as a feature.

Overall, his releases today were underwhelming. The first one I listened to was Habibti, nothing other than a slow sleep fest, the second one I think had higher highs than this one, and it was definitely better than the first one and then the whole Iceman album just seems boring and from what ive seen online, people agree.

I'm unsure where all the imagery and marketing that was there prior to the release went with all the cold and colourful stuff.

## Verdict

Underwhelming. Drake released 43 songs or so today and not a single one of them is mention-worthy or playlist-worthy, I don't think. I will have to relisten and I'll come back to this if I deem adjustments necessary.
