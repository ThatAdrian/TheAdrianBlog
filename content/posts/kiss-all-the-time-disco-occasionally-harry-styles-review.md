## The Sound

This album is my favourite by Harry Styles as i wasn't previously a fan, this album is very nice, it seems to hit every mood and vibe and it does them all quite well, starting with the single which was released previously, aperture which i believe is a decent track to release prior to the album as it teases the electronic vibe of the album quite well without revealing too much of the good stuff, and then when that one finishes and it goes into the banger of a song that American Girls is, the tracks i rated low aren't because they're bad, they're just boring. but songs like coming up roses and pop are extravagant songs and i highly recommend listening to them. 

I was made aware of this album by my lovely girlfriend, so thank her guys, i like it.

## Standout Tracks

American Girls, Coming Up Roses and Pop

## Verdict

Quite a pleasant album, again not revolutionary but i like seeing when mainstream artists branch out and do new things, i think harry executed the electronic sound quite well and i look forward to hearing what comes next from him.
