## The Sound

funky, upbeat, whatever, but super repetitive, vax found one style and is stuck to it, there is no variety in any of the tracks unfortunately. A lot of the singles he released are a lot better. It's not horrible by all means, just overdone already. I love Waeras ' production, but he needs to find some other producers also.

This album is digicore like rap with very melodic and electronic beats, vax has a quite unique vocal chain and method of singing, and I love his stutters. The songs individually are cool and unique from other music, but the songs on the album just meld in with each other. I'd compare it to Ken Carson's albums as for re-listen value, and I haven't listened to Ken Carson.

## Standout Tracks

Race with slayr and crushed

## Verdict

It's good background music, it sounds like one big song though, all blends in. I like the aesthetic and design of it though.
