## The Sound

This artist's music came up on shuffle a handful of times, and I tend to enjoy when it does so i decided to take a listen. The tracks are all similar but different enough to not feel too samey.

The sound is very similar to the Gunnr and Westwood side of the underground. very summery, creative upbeat instrumentals with neat transitions and a good voice over them.

The only reason why it's not higher is because some of the songs aren't crazy memorable (doesn't mean they're not good) and the sound/genre doesn't vary much throughout the album.

[TRACK_RATINGS]

## Verdict

Definitely needs one big track to blow up large scale but it's all about exposure now as the actual music is very good, the transitions are great, it's nice to sing along to and it's fun to listen to in the car. I recommend. I will be looking forward to further releases.
