## The Sound

Honestly, I listened to telephone and I love that song. I thought this guy was the main artist adn thought. "new album coming out soon, and I can pre-save it? Sure, why not? I like that song!" Turns out i might like Cece Natalie's contribution to it more than this.

See the beats themselves aren't bad apart from a couple songs with loud rolling hihats and completely randomly placed snares in some, they seem to get better towards the end of the album so if that's Elxnce's production, good job. If these beats were just bought, I'm disappointed.

Vocally, every song is underwhelming, overtuned voice, reverb and delay on each one and a lot of it; it feels whiny, and it feels like a temu bladee on his slow songs. The voice reminds me of Bladee and Quadeca in some songs, but incorrectly mixed with a bad vocal chain.

The ratings I'm giving here are quite loose, I listened to it fully in the car, and then I had to remember by flicking through what the difference is between each song while typing this up.

## Verdict

Maybe it's not for me, maybe my hopes were too high, maybe it is just bad, like the title says. If you get 3000 listens on your solo songs on the original release. Why release a deluxe??
