## The sound

Never really heard of Westwood prior to him popping up on my shuffle, some funky tracks. At first I thought he sounded like Tory Lanez and thought he took a huge spin after lost tapes or whatever his jail project was called. This Westwood fella has a great voice and it sounds great, props to him. the beats are funky and groovy, i can get down and have a little boogie to these and a good handful of these made it onto my playlist. theres also some pretty good transitions throughout it. I recommend as this sound is clean and somewhat unique.

[TRACK_RATINGS]

## Standout tracks

Truth, Ur All Mine, Overtime, Seperation and maybe Painless

## Verdict

I look forward to his future work and this album has good potential with the right exposure. If he has a tour id go to it and as I said a good handful made it onto my playlist, I saved the album so i will relisten.
