## The Sound

I think this is ACTUALLY his worst album yet. theres a running thing where people would say "this is the worst album yet" and then it grows on them slowly after a month or so. i gave it a chance to do that and it just didnt work.

So lets start off by putting his controversial presence as an artist to the side here and focus just on the discography and the tracks.

This album feels horribly plain and boring, not to mention it was delayed like a million times and what not, the tracks that were released initially which are highs and lows, preacher man, beauty and the beast and damn are the only real good tracks, i had a few of those on my playlist and i enjoyed them. i had high hopes for this album then he went ahead and ruined it with a ton of AI, then made a horrible marketing campaign around just AI visuals and so on and so on, a lot of these tracks sound badly mixed, repetitive, sleepy and just not passionate enough, there isnt anything of interest going on in a lot of it, just a flipped sample and some drums and it was called a day, the lyricism is lacking also.

I had tickets to wireless UK to see Ye perform as the performances look really good and entertaining but he managed to get his damn visa cancelled and therefore the whole festival was cancelled. i got my money back tho.
