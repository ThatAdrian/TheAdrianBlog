## The Sound

Ninajirachi pioneers the electronic space with this release and it's very obvious that everyone loves this release. I can't remember exactly when I first heard it but I think it's a great album and I have played it a hefty handful of times. Dare I say I do believe it is ever so slightly overhyped, considering the competition around it and the releases at the time but it is a great album nonetheless. 

I believe Nina won some large Australian award for this album, which is completely deserved. I'm kinda surprised that iPod Touch has more streams than fuck my computer but whatever the community prefers I guess.

I think both the Frost Children remix of fuck my computer and the Underscores remix of its you improve the songs in their own ways which is one reason why I don't rate those higher as they are literally improved by a better version of themselves.

[TRACK_RATINGS]

## Standout Tracks

Fuck my computer, ipod Touch, CSIRAC and Delete

## Verdict

Overall, a great release and exactly what I like to see, the production is amazing and I look forward to following along with future releases from Ninajirachi as this was my wakeup call to her music.
